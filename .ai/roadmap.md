# Project Roadmap

Proposed future milestones and enhancements for the OrderKart codebase:

## Milestone 1: P2P Sync Reliability (Current Focus)
- Validate hotspot connection handshakes under weak Wi-Fi signal.
- Expand incremental sync checks to prevent duplicate order stock deductions.

## Milestone 2: Maps and Route Enhancements
- Expand map picker UI inside customer creation.
- Support offline road calculation caches for worker visits.

## Milestone 3: Supplier and Warehouse Scaling
- Complete items warehouse allocation logic.
- Expand supplier price tracking history charts.
